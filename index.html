Driving Experience Assistant

The "Driving Experience Assistant" is a meticulously crafted web application designed to assist young drivers in recording their accompanied driving experiences. This project aims to provide a user-friendly platform for individuals undergoing accompanied driving to document and validate their driving milestones, ensuring they have accumulated a minimum of 3,000 km over a period of 2 years or more, with diverse driving conditions.
